About APAC

The ASIA PACIFIC AERONET CALIBRATION AND TRAINING CENTER is a regional center dedicated to enhancing atmospheric aerosol monitoring technologies. Our mission is to provide precise instrument calibration services to ensure data accuracy and consistency, while also offering advanced training programs for scientists, researchers, and technicians in the region. This helps strengthen capabilities in global climate change and air quality monitoring.
